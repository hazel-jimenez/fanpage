Bio

Jay Park was born in South Korea and his parents moved to Seattle when he was young, so that is where his interest for Hip Hop music begins.
He is a rapper, singer-songwriter, record producer, dancer, entrepreneur owning multiple record labels, and former K-Pop member.
He is one of the pioneers of commercializing Hip Hop or as they call it KHH (K- Hip Hop) in South Korea and making it okay for other
artist with the same passion and interest to be able to have a platform that fit and understood their needs.
